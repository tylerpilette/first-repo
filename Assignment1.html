    How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Pick out some bread
  2. Spread out some mayo or mustard
  3. Pick out some veggies (lettuce, pickles, tomatoes, onions)
  4. Consider cheese (Swiss, cheddar, gouda, American)
  5. Add a meat(or protein) (pastrami, turkey, salami, bologna)
  6. Put everything between two slices of bread and eat!
